Gin Rummy

How to Play Gin Rummy

A two-player card game of skill and strategy. First to 100 points wins.

The Goal

Reduce the value of your unmatched cards — called deadwood — to 10 points or fewer, then knock. The player with less deadwood scores the difference. First to reach 100 points wins.

Card Values

Ace 1 pt
2 – 10 Face value
J, Q, K 10 pts each

Melds

Melded cards don't count as deadwood. There are two types:

Set
3 or 4 cards of the same rank
e.g. 8♥ 8♦ 8♠
Run
3 or more consecutive cards of the same suit
e.g. 3♥ 4♥ 5♥

⚠️ Aces are low only — A♠ 2♠ 3♠ is valid, but Q♠ K♠ A♠ is not.

Taking a Turn

1
Draw one card — either the top card from the stock pile, or the top card from the discard pile.
2
Discard one card by dragging it to the discard pile. This ends your turn.
3
Optionally, knock instead of discarding — if your deadwood is 10 or fewer points.

Knocking

When you knock, the game automatically finds the best melds in both hands and calculates deadwood. Three outcomes are possible:

Gin 🎉
You have zero deadwood. You score the difference in deadwood plus a 25-point bonus.
Knock ✊
Your deadwood is less than your opponent's. You score the difference in deadwood.
Undercut 😬
Your opponent's deadwood is equal to or less than yours. They score the difference plus a 25-point bonus.

Tips

Use the Sort by Suit button to spot runs, and Sort by Value to spot sets. You can also drag cards within your hand to arrange them however you like. The newly drawn card is highlighted briefly so you always know what you just picked up.
